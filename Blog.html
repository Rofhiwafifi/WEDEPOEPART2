HOW TO PREPARE FOR JOB SHADOWING

By Admin| August 22, 2025

Job Shadowing is one of the best ways to understand how a career works in real life. To prepare, start by:

  • 1. Researching the company and the role you will be shadowing.
  • 2. Dress appropriately and bring a notebook to jot down key insights.
  • 3. Be curious- ask questions politely and show enthusiasm.
  • 4. Most importantly, reflect afterwards on what you learned and how it fits your career goals.

    Starting your career journey as a student can feel overwhelming, but small steps can make a big difference. You may use these steps as a guide:
    1. Focus on building your skills, not just getting grades.
    2. Volunteer or join clubs to gain experience.
    3. Network with teachers, professionals and peers.
    4. Keep your CV updated.
    5. Dont be afraid to explore different career paths, you dont have to have it all figured out yet.

    WHY JOB SHADOWING MATTERS

    By Guest Writer| August 22,2025

    Many students think that job shadowing is just about watching someone work but it is much more than that. Job shadowing helps you see day-to-day reality of a profession, the callenges people face and the skill that you need. It allows you to test whether a career is right for you before investing years of study. For employers, it is also a chance to meet motivated students who could become future employees.
